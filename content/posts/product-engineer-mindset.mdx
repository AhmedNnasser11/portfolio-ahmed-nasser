Being a product engineer means more than just writing clean code. It's about understanding the user's needs and translating them into a seamless experience.

### Aesthetics Matter

A great product should not only work well but also look and feel premium. This is why I focus on:

- Typography
- Spacing
- Micro-interactions

> "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." - Steve Jobs
